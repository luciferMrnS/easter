About Us

We are just some high-spirited cousins from a part of Nigeria where Yoruba is beauty, Pidgin is merry, English mmmm packaging (maybe). Back to the storyline, so yeah, we decided one day that we should spend time with our mutual grandma during a particular easter, many years down the line now. It was adventurous, beautiful - that memory... there was no coming back from that, so it became a custom. We got high on our discovery, we couldn't keep it to ourselves so we gave some friends in crime a sneak peek of what easters look like for us and guess what - these partners in crime won't stay put when easter is around the corner, they always ready to board that easter train with us down to grandma's. Years roll over years and one thing became evident, this isn't just a discovery anymore, it's a movement. Our numbers grew drastically, interests from people hit us like a plague we can't afford, we storm grandma's hometown with exceptionality and valor. Ok make I calm down, English don dey drop but the whole gist here is - easter is always fun, just wine and dine with the right people, and you don't need to keep looking, we are here, the right people you need to have the right kinda easter. Begin this year, but I promise you, there won't be an end to that beginning.
